Bio Cleaner - Is it Safe For the Food?

The Bio Cleaner is a unique multipurpose cleaning agent, formulated with necessary bacteria that break down grease, soap scum, grime, dirty water and dirt, thus making cleaning faster, safer and easier.

This unique cleaning agent also has the ability to kill germs and bacteria. It has been tested by consumers, who found it to be of high quality and an effective cleaner. It was created as a result of the growing demands for a cleaner, which could be used in many areas of our homes such as bathrooms and kitchens.

The main purpose of cleaning agents like the Bio Cleaner is to help eliminate germs and bacteria that cause illnesses in our homes.
Thus, we all need to make our homes germ free and clean at all times.
The invention of this unique and advanced cleaning solution came about after researches and studies showed that certain components are vital for killing germs and bacteria in kitchen and bathroom surfaces and other surfaces throughout the house including the laundry room and basements.

A number of solutions were tried and tested but only the natural bio cleaner proved to be effective, safe and efficient. This cleaner contains Echinaceaptomophane which has antibacterial, anti-microbial and antifungal properties. In fact, it is already used in many hospitals worldwide and is found to be very useful in fighting illnesses such as cough, cold and flu.

As mentioned earlier, it is also useful in disinfecting kitchen and bathroom surfaces. It is non-toxic, so there are no harmful effects on children or animals when used for cleaning.
