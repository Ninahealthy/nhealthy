Healthcare Providers Benefit From AthenaHealth Solutions

The need for qualified, competent and compassionate healthcare providers has increased at an alarming rate in recent years. The aging Baby Boomers are living longer and are in greater demand than ever before. This has resulted in a surplus of health care professionals nationwide that are required to meet the demand. Athenahealth Providers are qualified professionals who work directly with the patients to ensure their needs are met. Providers work as direct outsourced nurses or clinical support specialist, within a private, non-profit organization.The organization is organized around three key principles which work towards providing patients with exceptional healthcare. The first is providing personalized patient care based on each patient's unique health history and condition. The second is offering payment plans for services rendered. The third principle is maintaining a high level of integrity throughout the health care system. Athenahealth providers are required to meet stringent requirements for credentialing, and ongoing training.The benefits of this service are two fold. Firstly, with the use of a schedule optimization solution, providers are able to improve their bottom line by decreasing staff levels. This means more income for the organization per month. Secondly, because of the use of predictive analytics, patients are being seen and treated more quickly and efficiently. The end result is less lost time for the patient and quicker diagnosis and care.Each patient has a completely customized portal to their online health portal at home. The first such platform is called the patient experience platform. This is an online application which allows the patients to access their own portal. This is where they can make their appointments, manage their accounts and monitor their treatments. On the other end, healthcare providers have the opportunity to access patient information. By using the analytics provided through the platform they can see how often a particular procedure is performed, what time the patient is in the office, how many appointments are made during a given day, the type of treatment and if any follow up procedures need to be done.

To make improvements to their businesses, healthcare providers athens Healthcare requires regular updates to the software used in their system. This includes scheduling and rescheduling procedures as well as patient education about their healthcare. The Athens Health Information Management Team (HIMT) was established to partner with health care providers to create user friendly and convenient software solutions. Their mission is to offer superior patient care by combining technology and strategy. Through HIPT, providers have access to real time data, detailed information and detailed reports about each procedure which can then be used to make effective scheduling decisions.The goal of athenahealth and its partners is to continuously improve quality and service while reducing costs. Through their comprehensive portfolio of digital platform solutions and their rigorous patient satisfaction metrics, healthcare providers athens are taking the guesswork out of scheduling and rescheduling. They use their flexible infrastructure to provide easy accessibility to various software products that can be customized to meet their individual business needs. Through this flexibility athenahealth allows for immediate innovation and engagement of new and current practices in order to increase efficiency and minimize errors.Other benefits offered by the athenahealth platform include: improved communication between staff and patients and reduced costs and errors. They use state of the art technology to deliver personalized service which is essential in maintaining a healthy work environment. Through the clinical information portal, healthcare providers can monitor and track patient demographics and manage the records remotely. The platform also includes a variety of other features that streamline patient management, including appointment reminders, appointment setting, medication reminders, immunization reminders, and more. Athenahealth is constantly improving its technology to offer more convenience to patients and maximize productivity.Athenahealth provides complete solutions to its clients and partners through their hosted Software as a Service (SaaS) and cloud-based enterprise architecture. The platform is designed to enable collaboration between athenahealth providers and healthcare consumers at the enterprise level. This enables better information exchange that reduces errors and increases quality and productivity. Athenahealth's hosted SaaS platform empowers users to create and maintain multiple data centers that can be customized for optimal allocation of resources. Athenahealth also offers consulting services to its clients to help them implement their technology and obtain maximum return on their investments.
